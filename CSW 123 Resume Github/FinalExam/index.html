First Article

“The user diversity in terms of network conditions and device capabilities, which accompanies the world-wide prosperity of various video services, poses challenges for video service providers to provide satisfactory Quality of Experience (QoE), e.g., preventing video quality oscillation and even video freezing. The case becomes more severe for mobile users with time-varying wireless channels and highly diverse hardware/- software specifications [2].
